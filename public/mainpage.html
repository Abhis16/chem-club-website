OHS Chemistry Club

Who are we

We are the OHS Chemistry Club. A group of around 7 individuals from freshman to seniors interested in the study of chemistry.

What do we do?

We study chemistry! From general chemistry outside of AP Chem (but in the class it replaces) to organic chemistry to all sorts of random things, we study anything related to chemistry. Occasionally we also study things totally outside of chemistry too.

How can I join?

Show up!

We generally meet Mondays after school until 4 in Mrs. Canestraight's room. You can email us at ohschemistryclub@gmail.com!
